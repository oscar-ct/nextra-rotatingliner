# Traction

## 2018

Began preliminary testing on RLE and baseline engine to determine friction reduction estimates and face seal reliability

## 2021

Preliminary testing confirmed face seal reliability under high pressure and fuel consumption benefits of up to 40% at idle conditions

## 2022

Testing under approximately 30% load proved fuel economy benefit of 12% - 14%

## 2023

Interest expressed by a major Tier I manufacturer, Mahle
Current - Validating fuel economy benefits at higher loads
